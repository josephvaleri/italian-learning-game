📚 How to Use the Italian Learning Game

🔢 Learn Your Numbers

  • See an Italian number word
  • Choose the correct number from 4 options
  • Practice 10 random numbers each session

🎯 Speech Shadow

  • See an Italian word or phrase
  • Type the English translation
  • Click "Show Answer" if you need help
  • Practices vocab, phrases, and idioms

🌉 Word Bridge

  • See an English word or phrase
  • Choose the correct Italian translation
  • Multiple choice with 4 options
  • Instant feedback on each answer

🚗 Driver's License Test

  • 30 questions from official Italian exam
  • 20-minute timer (turns red at 1 minute)
  • Answer VERO (true) or FALSO (false)
  • Pass with ≤3 errors
  • Review wrong answers at the end

📖 Verb Master

  • See an Italian verb infinitive
  • Choose the correct English meaning
  • Practice 10 random verbs each session
  • From a database of 1,000 common verbs
